Welcome To The Jutland Art Academy.

The Jutland Art Academy is an academy that educates artists. The academy's activities are aimed at creating an artistically developing and intellectually stimulating study community. The academy works on the basis that visual art does not work without an investigative dialogue with political, technological and economic conditions. The academy has a very industrialist aesthetic. It has graffiti on the walls and is full of artistic expression. An environment like this also offers a courtyard where people can enjoy themselves watching students create their art. Various art exhibitions are also open for the public to enjoy.
