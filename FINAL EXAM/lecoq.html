Welcome To Lecoq.

In the cozy Latin Quarter, exactly in the street Graven, you will find Lecoq. One of the hip bars in Aarhus that you will become addicted to. Lecoq is divided into two, with a pub in one room and a restaurant in the other. The atmosphere is informal, and the guests show up as they are and in high spirits. There is room for everyone here, and you get along with each other. If you smoke, you can stay at one end of the bar.

At Lecoq you will find a cozy and informal atmosphere with room for everyone. The atmosphere is often high and there are many people. The inn is divided into several departments, which seem to have a life of their own. You can also take a seat at the bar.
