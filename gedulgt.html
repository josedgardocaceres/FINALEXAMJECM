Welcome To Gedulgt.

Gedulgt is one of the best cocktail bars in Aarhus. Well hidden in a backyard in Fredensgade, you have to know it's there. At Gedulgt, they make cocktails that have to be experienced. Here is something for the eye and especially something for the taste. It is an experience to study how the bartenders work with cocktails and make them appear completely unique with smoke and colors. Gedulgt is not just an ordinary cocktail bar. It was voted "Best Cocktail Bar In Denmark People's Choice" in 2022, and one of the bartenders was also awarded the title of "Best Bartender in Denmark". Gedulgt is open Thursday to Saturday. There is a special atmosphere at Gedulgt. Stylish and cosy. You can take a seat at the long bar and keep an eye on the skilled bartenders, or take a seat at one of the tables.
